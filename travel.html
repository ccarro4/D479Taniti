Island Exploration


Around the Island

Traveling around an island offers a variety of convenient options to explore its beauty. Public transportation, such as buses and shuttles, provide affordable ways to get around popular spots. Taxis and ride-sharing services offer quick and flexible transportation, while rental cars give visitors the freedom to explore remote areas at their own pace. For a more leisurely experience, rental bicycles are perfect for enjoying scenic routes and discovering hidden gems along the coast. With these options, exploring the island becomes easy and enjoyable for every traveler.


Air Travel

Using small aircraft to reach an island offers several advantages. It provides faster travel times compared to ferries or boats, allowing travelers to maximize their time exploring the destination. The scenic flight offers breathtaking views of the ocean and island landscapes, making the journey itself a memorable part of the trip. Overall, small aircraft provide a convenient, efficient, and scenic way to arrive at Taniti.


Cruise Ship

Arriving by cruise ship offers a luxurious and scenic experience. Passengers can enjoy stunning ocean views during the voyage and arrive directly at a picturesque port, often close to the island’s main attractions. The cruise ship provides comfort and convenience, with onboard amenities and entertainment, making the journey as enjoyable as the destination itself.
