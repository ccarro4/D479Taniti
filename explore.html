Getting Around


Island Adventures

Island activities offer an adventure-filled escape, including exhilarating volcano hikes that reveal stunning lava landscapes and panoramic views. Visitors can also soar through the rainforest on zip lines, enjoying the lush canopy and vibrant wildlife from above. For a truly spectacular experience, helicopter rides provide sweeping aerial views of the island’s volcanic peaks, dense forests, and pristine coastlines, making every moment an unforgettable exploration of nature’s wonders.


Water Adventures

Activities such as chartered fishing give visitors the opportunity to catch local fish in picturesque waters, suitable for all skill levels. Snorkeling lets guests discover vibrant coral reefs and colorful marine life just below the surface. For a burst of excitement, jet skiing offers a thrilling way to glide across the waves and take in the island’s breathtaking coastline. These experiences provide an adventurous and enjoyable way to explore the island’s stunning natural beauty.


City Sights

Exploring a tropical island city offers a vibrant mix of sights and activities. Visitors can discover local microbreweries that craft unique island flavors. The city’s lively arcades provide fun entertainment, and its galleries and museums showcase the rich culture and history of the area. Whether strolling through bustling markets or relaxing at seaside cafes, there's always something exciting to see and do on this tropical urban paradise.
