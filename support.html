Frequently Asked Questions
  • Can I book excursions after I arrive?
    Yes. You are welcome to add, change, or exchange any of your excursions after your initial booking. Please be aware of any price changes and any deposits which may apply, however.
  • If I'm not happy with my lodgings, can I change them?
    Yes, depending on availability. Each of the island's hotels, bungalows, AirBnB locations and other lodgings are owned and operated individually. You will need to contact the properties to arrange your lodgings if you wish to alter them after your arrival. Please be aware of price differences and any deposits.
  • My question isn't answered here and I'd like to speak to someone directly, how can I do that?
    You can call our Travel Agents directly at +99-555-555-5555, we are staffed around the clock for your convenience and have support in many languages.
